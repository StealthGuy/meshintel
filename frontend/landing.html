10 Strategic Insights: Why Mesh Intelligence Matters

visibility

Rendering the Invisible Visible

In a LoRa network, topology is a ghost. MeshIntel transforms invisible radio signals into coherent structural maps, allowing you to see the true shape of your off-grid infrastructure.

route

The Silent Tax of Hops

Every hop is a gamble with time and battery life. Analyzing Average Path Length is the only way to measure the vital efficiency of your communication.

hub

Identifying Structural Breakpoints

A mesh is only as strong as its most critical node. Through Betweenness Centrality, we pinpoint bottlenecks before a single failure isolates entire communities.

warning

Simulating Failure for Resilience

Security is a process, not a state. With our Attack Simulation, we stress-test network robustness by removing critical hubs to see if the mesh survives or fragments.

groups

Communities, Not Just Nodes

Mesh networks are social infrastructures. Louvain and Leiden algorithms reveal how nodes naturally cluster, helping to coordinate regional network leaders.

analytics

Spectrum Health Analysis

Real-time monitoring of Channel Utilization and Airtime TX. We prevent spectrum congestion by identifying nodes that risk frequency saturation and blocking emergency traffic.
